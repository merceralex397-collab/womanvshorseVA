## Fix Summary
Regex in `.opencode/tools/smoke_test.ts` line 584 changed from `/Failed to load script "res:\/\//i` to `/Failed to load script/i`. This ensures fatal script-load errors (exit_code=0, stderr contains "Failed to load script") are classified as `syntax_error` (blocking) instead of `tooling_parse_warning` (non-blocking).
